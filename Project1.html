Question List

  1. What was your first impression of this site?
  2. How does this site organize the flow of information?
  3. Does the site structure make sense or confuse you? Why?
  4. Do the graphics/layout/multimedia enhance the site or detract from it? Why?
  5. How long did it take for the home page to load? Was the load time reasonable?
  6. Was the text color and size readable? Did the background help or hurt the site?
  7. Did the site have a navigation bar? Was it well done?
  8. Did the site have a repeated color scheme or look and feel?
  9. How does the site respond to smaller window sizes?
  10. What was unusual or very well done on this site?
